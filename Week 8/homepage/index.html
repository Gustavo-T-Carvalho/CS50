Descrição

Hello, my name is Gustavo, and I currently work as a Software Engineer at SYDLE, focusing on billing, CRM, and BPMN solutions using the SYDLE ONE platform. I work with technologies such as JavaScript, Angular, HTML, CSS, Elasticsearch, MongoDB, Git, and agile methodologies (Scrum and Kanban). Additionally, I have experience with the React.js framework and solid knowledge of Node.js, Java, and C#. I hold a postgraduate degree in Fullstack Development from XP Educação, a degree in Civil Engineering from the Federal University of Viçosa, and a technical degree in Computer Science and Systems Development from CEFET-MG.
